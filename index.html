What We Do

Hardware Design

We engineer custom high-performance PCBs, integrating dual-core SoCs like the nRF5340 alongside advanced analog front-ends like the MAX30001G for real-time biopotential and ECG acquisition.

Software Dev

From low-level embedded C++ managing Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) stacks to scalable React Native mobile applications, we build the robust logic behind the hardware.

Community

Hosting design workshops, intensive hackathons, and industry networking events to connect engineering students directly with the future of biomedical technology.
