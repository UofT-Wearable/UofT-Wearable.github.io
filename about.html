Our Mission

UofT Wearable is a design team dedicated to advancing wearable technology by developing novel wearable ideas using state-of-the-art methods.

Wearables are a rapidly growing market, and we hope to use the wearable platform to increase consumer access to cutting-edge technologies. We are always open to your ideas and finding new ways to bring them to reality.
